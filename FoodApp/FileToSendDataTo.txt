<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ingredients"> Ingredients </span></h2><ul><li> 2 medium-large <a href="/wiki/Orange" title="Orange">oranges</a></li><li> 2 <a href="/wiki/Lemon" title="Lemon">lemons</a></li><li> 1 small box <a href="/wiki/Raisins" title="Raisins">raisins</a> (about 1? ounces [40g])</li><li> 8 oz (220g) <a href="/wiki/Honey" title="Honey">honey</a></li><li> 12 oz (355 ml) <a href="/wiki/Whiskey" title="Whiskey">whiskey</a> (or more, to taste) <sup id="cite_ref-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-0">[1]</a></sup>4 Queens Whiskey</li><li> ? to 1 teaspoon each of any or all of these spices: <a href="/wiki/Cinnamon" title="Cinnamon">cinnamon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nutmeg" title="Nutmeg">nutmeg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cloves" title="Cloves">cloves</a>, <a href="/wiki/Caraway_seed" title="Caraway seed">caraway seed</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anise_seed" title="Anise seed">aniseed</a></li></ul><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Directions"> Directions </span></h2><ol><li> Peel the oranges and lemons.</li><li> Cut up the fruit and squeeze them into the crock pot.</li><li> A garlic press works, or some kind of juicer or fruit squeezer.</li><li> Put the remaining fruit pulp into the crock pot as well.</li><li> Add the raisins, honey, and spices.</li><li> Stir.</li><li> <i><b>note</b>: do not add whiskey yet!</i> If you cook the whiskey, even for a short time, the alcohol will start to evaporate. Start the crock pot and let the mixture cook for about 2 Ð 4 hours.</li><li> Stir occasionally.</li><li> It's complete when the fruit pulp gets cooked down.</li><li> Strain into a pitcher. Mash down the fruit in the strainer to get all the liquid.</li><li> Now add the whiskey to the pitcher and stir.</li><li> Taste, and add more whiskey to your liking.</li><li> Serve hot in shot glasses, espresso cups, or coffee mugs.</li><li> After the first round, each individual serving can be heated in the microwave.</li></ol><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes"> Notes </span></h2><ol class="references"><li id="cite_note-0"><a href="#cite_ref-0">?</a> <span class="reference-text">This recipe makes about 12 ounces (355 mL) of "virgin" boilo. Add 12 ounces of whiskey to this and you get 24 ounces (710 mL) of Coal Region Nectar, enough for 12-18 servings.</span></li></ol>
